# Incident Runbook - First 60 Minutes

## 0-10 Minutes
- Confirm incident severity and assign incident commander.
- Open communication channel and status thread.

## 10-30 Minutes
- Isolate failing component and apply immediate containment.
- Record timeline and actions.

## 30-60 Minutes
- Validate service recovery.
- Define customer communication.
- Create post-incident action list with owners.
